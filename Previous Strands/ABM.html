Associated Strands and Career

Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM)

The ABM strand is designed for students who are interested in pursuing careers in business, finance, marketing, and management. This strand provides a solid foundation in business principles, accounting, economics, and entrepreneurship, preparing students for various roles in the corporate world.

What You Will Study?

▶
  • Accountancy: Principles of accounting, financial accounting, and cost accounting.
  • Business: Business management, entrepreneurship, and marketing.
  • Management: Organizational behavior, human resource management, and strategic planning.
  • Economics: Principles of economics, microeconomics, and macroeconomics.

Key Skills You Will Develop

▶
  • Analytical & Critical Thinking: Analyzing case studies and making informed business decisions.
  • Problem-solving: Handling real-world business challenges.
  • Financial Literacy: Managing finances effectively.
  • Communication: Presenting ideas and leading teams.

Jobs And Careers

▶

Accountant: Accountants record, analyze, and interpret financial transactions. They ensure compliance with tax laws and regulations and provide financial insights to help businesses make informed decisions.

Financial Analyst: Financial analysts evaluate financial data to help businesses make informed decisions. They analyze market trends, assess investment opportunities, and provide recommendations.

Marketing Manager: Marketing managers develop and implement marketing strategies to promote products or services. They analyze market trends, identify target audiences, and coordinate marketing campaigns.

Entrepreneur: An entrepreneur is a visionary individual who identifies a market need or opportunity, then creates and launches a new business or venture, taking on financial risks to develop innovative products or services, manage operations, and earn profits. They are risk-takers, innovators, and leaders who organize resources, face uncertainty, and drive economic growth by introducing new solutions.

HR Specialist: HR specialists manage human resources within organizations. They handle recruitment, employee relations, benefits administration, and ensure compliance with labor laws.

Business Consultant: Business consultants provide expert advice to organizations to improve performance and efficiency. They analyze business processes, identify areas for improvement, and implement strategic solutions.
